📱 Check your phones first
Before you start posting, open the Digital tab and update each phone's status — tap to set connected, disconnected, or issue.

Disconnected phones are excluded from today's targets and the end-of-day report, so keeping them accurate matters.

• record today's completion, and
• permanently remove all posted (green) items from the sourcing queue.
